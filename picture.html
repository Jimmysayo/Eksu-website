Chelsea

Chelsea Football Club is a professional football club based in Fulham, West London, England.
They compete in the Premier League, the top tier of English football.
Founded in 1905, the team play their home games at Stamford Bridge.
The club won their first major honour, the League championship, in 1955.

Chelsea FC was founded in 1905 by Henry Augustus Mears.
Home games are played at Stamford Bridge stadium, the original site Mears chose for the club.
The team had mixed fortunes over the years, not winning a major trophy until the 1950s
and moving up and down between divisions several times
